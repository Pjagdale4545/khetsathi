Our Mission

At **Khetsathi** (meaning "Farmer's Friend"), our mission is to empower farmers by providing them with the right tools and information. From managing your crops and getting weather alerts to securing loans and finding the best market prices, we are here to support you every step of the way.

We believe in a future where technology makes farming smarter, more efficient, and more profitable for everyone.
